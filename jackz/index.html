Welcome to Jackz

Jackz is a start up company focused on bringing the best experience for you and your beloved dog. How do we do this you ask? The answer is simple. Through our loving products and confidence building, enriching barnhunt Trials along side NWA Barnhunt.

The treat bags designed and produced by Jackz is the perfect companion to any aspiring or established dog trainer. With its removable pouches you can easily swap treats for any given occasion or training session. The removable treat pouches not only allow for easy washing, but also allows for easy storage!

NWA Barnhunt

NWA Barnhunt is a recently opened barn hunt club based in Northwest Arkansas that is providing more chances to everyone around to experience the enriching activities of barnhunt. Together with Jackz anybody can sign up for barnhunt lessons to prepare for any upcoming trials or just for the enrichment of their dog!

What is Barnhunt?

Barnhunt is a dog sport in which your dog can compete by finding several rats inside of tubes hidden within a straw obsticle course. Barnhunt has several levels in which your dog can compete, including: Novice, Open, Senior, Master and Crazy 8s. Click here to learn more and sign up for a lesson!
